This is Our story

We delivers best authentic food, yet elegantly modern take on Indian cuisine. It is inspired to share love for the food and culture of native India, assembled a talented team to create a world-class restaurant that would re-define how Indian cuisine is presented on the international gastronomic stage. A restaurant that is the recipient of a prestigious Michelin star the year it opened and has earned the same coveted award every year since its 2010 debut. The team shares a vision of the future for Indian cuisine, an endeavor to improve the purest traditional and regional cuisines, while simultaneously creating innovative, modern dishes and interpretation. Our passion is a contemporary restaurant offering dining experiences to satisfy all aspects of fine cuisine, in a vibrant and trendsetting ambience. It is a place of gathering, a place of celebration, a place for business and a place for pleasure.

Our Cultural Beliefs

We’re in the business of making people feel special. In everything we do, we have to ask ourselves if we’re putting the Guest first and making sure they’ll come back for more.
